About Toronto Homicides

This map visualizes homicide data across Toronto neighborhoods for the year 2024. The data is categorized into four groups: 0 homicides, 1-2 homicides, 3-4 homicides, and 5 or more homicides. The goal of this visualization is to provide insights into crime patterns and help identify areas that may require additional attention.
